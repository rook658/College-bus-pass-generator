  1. About the website
    • CET bus pass portal is designed to digitalize the process of getting the bus pass.
    • It simplifies the process of applying and recieving the pass for both staffs and students. this ensures transparency efficiency,accessibility and easy updation.
  2. Objectives of Website
    • Anyone can request for bus pass from anywhere
    • know your transport details accurately
    • Can avoid queues and paperwork
    • recieve instant Updates and notifications
    • easy renewal process
  3. Eligibility and rules
    • Bus passes are available for all students and staffs
    • Passes will be valid for 3 months or 6 months
    • All updates will be posted in the Updates section of this website
    • If you have any doubts,refer the contact page
      Click here to go to Contacts
    • Lost passes can be reissued with penalty fee
  4. How it works
    • Go to apply page and fill the Application
    • Then proceed to pay and pay using available options
    • download the reciept
    • Recieve the generated buspass from bus office after 7 working days once its verified
