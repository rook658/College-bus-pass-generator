FAQs

  1. Application and Registration
    • How do I apply for a new bus pass?

      • Go to the Apply page, fill in your details, select your route, and submit the form.

    • Can I edit my details after submitting the form?

      • No, but you can contact the transport office or raise a support ticket for corrections.

    • I did not receive my application confirmation â€” what should I do?

      • Check your spam folder. If not found, raise a ticket with your register number and email.

    • Can I apply for more than one route?

      • No. Each student is allowed to select only one bus route.

  2. Payment and fees
    • What should I do if my payment fails?

      • Wait a few minutes and check your bank account. If the amount was deducted but not reflected, raise a ticket with a screenshot of the transaction.

    • Is the bus fee refundable?

      • No, the bus fee is non-refundable once payment is confirmed.

    • Can I pay the fee offline?

      • No, currently only online payments through the portal are accepted.

  1. Routes and pass
    • How can I know which route to choose?

      • you can choose the route by clicking on the dropdown menus in the apply page

    • What if I board a different route bus by mistake?

      • Show your pass to the conductor and inform the bus office later.

    • Can I change my route after getting the pass?

      • Yes, but only during the renewal period and with proper approval.

  2. Pass validity and renewal
    • How long is the bus pass valid?

      • Bus pass is valid for 6 or 3 months from date of issues

    • how to renew the pass?

      • Click on the renew option and fill the necessary details and wait till the confirmation form bus ofiice.

  3. Response
    • when will i get the bus pass after payment?

      • it usually takes 7 working days to issue the bus pass.

      • yes, you can.
